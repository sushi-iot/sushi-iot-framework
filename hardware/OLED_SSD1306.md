# <u>**OLED display**</u>
![OLED SSD1306](OLED_SSD1306.png)

0.96" OLED display, with SSD1315 or SSD1306 controller.  

Pinout:
* GND
* VCC/VDD: 3.3-5V (check your module data sheet !)
* SCL: I2C clock line
* SDA: I2C data line

**Sourcing**
